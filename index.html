What's inside

Everything you need to train with intent.

From muscle to movement to measurable progress — Gym Trainer keeps the whole loop in one focused app.

Interactive 3D muscle map

Rotate between front and back, tap any muscle group, and instantly see every exercise that targets it.

Smart routines

Filter by Push, Pull, Legs or Full Body and build sessions that match the way you actually split your week.

Guided sessions & rest timer

Log sets and weights as you go, with a wall-clock rest timer and optional audio cues so you never lose your rhythm.

Progress & personal records

Track streaks, weekly volume and per-exercise PRs so every session builds on the last.

kg / lb & light/dark

Switch units on the fly and pick the theme that suits your gym lighting — your preferences, your way.

Private by design

No account, no ads, no tracking. Your workout history, records and settings live on your device — not in the cloud.

Privacy

Your training data never leaves your phone.

Gym Trainer doesn't ask you to sign up, doesn't run ads, and doesn't track you. Workouts, personal records and settings are stored locally on your device. The app only ever downloads the public exercise catalog — it never uploads anything about you.
